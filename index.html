Give your students a great head start with our free Higher Education support sessions.

Want practical help to guide your students as they leave school? Every member of our Outreach Team is a former careers advisor and can give you all the support you need.

Choosing which path to take after school can be a difficult decision for students. As their teacher or careers advisor, your role can make a big difference. That’s why we work closely with schools, as well as parents.

Free support sessions

We can run free support sessions for students at your school, tailored to suit their needs, from a simple presentation to a thought-provoking, interactive workshop. Whichever approach is right for you, we’ll introduce your students to the exciting world of Higher Education and give them the skills they need to successfully apply for a place.

Our sessions have been developed with our industry partners as well as academic experts, so they’ll also introduce your students to the real world of work and careers.

What do our sessions cover?

  • Employability skills
  • A day in the life of a business professional
  • Business workshops
  • Which degree and university is right for me?
  • Why Pearson College London?
  • Personal statement clinic
  • Can you manage your money?
  • Parents’evenings      

Plus anything else you’d like us to include.

Degree taster days

We can organise degree taster days for your students, at our offices on The Strand in London. Giving them the chance to find out for themselves what life and learning is like here. They’ll also be able to talk to our students and lecturers. 

Higher Education and careers events

We’d be happy to attend your Higher Education and careers events, from having a stall to organising taster activities on your behalf.

Why Pearson College London?

We’re the only FTSE 100 company offering a range of undergraduate degrees in the UK. Our students graduate with a full honours degree validated by Royal Holloway and Bedford New College, which is part of the University of London and ranked in the top 1% of universities in the world. They also graduate with a Pearson College Diploma, a competence-based qualification that focuses on getting students ready for the workplace.

Amazing industry experience

As the name behind the Financial Times and Penguin, our students get a unique insight into the real world of work, and develop the skills and qualities they’ll need to succeed in today’s tough graduate job market.

Our degrees are designed in collaboration with an outstanding list of industry partners, including Sony Pictures, L’Oreal, BT, Cisco, the Peter Jones Foundation and Atos. That means your students will get direct exposure to hugely successful businesses and business leaders, through weekly industry workshops, termly industry conferences and a yearly residential event.

They’ll be taught by industry professionals as well as leading academics, in the heart of central London, and get the chance to benefit from extraordinary internships and work placements.

Our students start their career from day one

Our students work with a Talent Coach and industry-based mentor to develop their career from the very start of their degree, through one-to-one careers support and training. We help them develop the kind of skills and qualities employers really want, known as the 12 core competencies, plus the art of professional communication.

Financial support

Our students are entitled to apply for Government funding for their tuition fees and a maintenance loan. We also offer an attractive range of scholarships and bursaries, to make sure talented students with potential can succeed on their degrees, regardless of their financial situation.
